My Lovely Bearded Dragon New

Sunday, Oct 25 2020


Hello guys! So I would like to introduce my pet to you guys, her name is Citri. I call her Citri because She has a citrus color.

She is a bearded dragon. Have you ever heard about this reptile? Bearded dragon is a semi-arboreal Australian lizard with spiny scales and a large throat pouch bearing sharp spines. Citri has a Hypo morph. There are a lot of morph of Bearded Dragon such as Dunner, Leatherback, Silkback, etc. Her length is now about 25 cm, She loves eating Dubia Cockroaches!
